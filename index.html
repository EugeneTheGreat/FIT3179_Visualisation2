THE World University Rankings

From 2011 - 2023

The Times Higher Education (THE) World University Rankings provides definitive source of data on higher education in the world. It is the only measurement evaluating universities for teaching, research, knowledge transfer, and international outlook while also emphasising on the universities' research. THE uses 13 carefully calibrated performance metrics to provide the most comprehensive and balanced comparisons, which are grouped into 5 areas namely, Teaching, Research, Citations, International Outlook, and Industry Income. Thus, it is one of the most popular and widely used world university rankings in the education industry.

Distribution of Universities

The university distribution is represented by the percentage of ranked universities the country has for the selected year. United States of America and United Kingdom have always been the leading country in having high ranked university counts throughout the years.
